We feature a range of very talented local bands playing great jazz music in a relaxed yet vibrant environment. The regular jazz nights feature a sit-in bracket and dancers are most welcome.
Meals and bar facility are available.
Find out more...

One of the aims of the Club is the identification and development of younger musicians interested in playing jazz music. To this end the Club has begun hosting a Junior Jammers evening, basically every second Friday evening, at the Lounge Bar of the Sugar Shaker (Grand Chancellor Hotel) from 6pm to 9pm. There is a door charge of $10 pp (cash only).
